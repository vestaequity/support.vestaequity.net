      How to buy USDC with Pera Algo Wallet

      Follow the guide below to purchase USDC using Pera Wallet & Transak

      Start by opening your Pera Algo Wallet 

      • Select the "Buy/Sell" button located on the Home tab.
      • Select "Buy USDC with Transak".

                                     

       

        Select your Account & Payment Method

      • Select your desired Wallet Account and the desired method of payment you'd like to proceed with. 
      • Keep in mind there may be variable fees depending on your desired payment method.                  

             

       

      Review details and proceed with the payment

      • Please confirm all details are correct, and select "Buy USDC".
      • Once finalized, all USDC will be deposited into the selected Pera Algo Wallet account.

       

      Great work, you're ready to invest!

      • Visit your Dashboard to confirm your wallet has been successfully funded. 
      • Once confirmed, visit the Marketplace to start making offers. 

       

      Our Customer Success team is here to help, so if you have any questions at all, please feel free to reach out to schedule a time to chat. Customer Success Calendar
