      How to setup and connect your Pera Wallet!

      Easily setup and connect your Pera Algo Wallet to Vesta Equity!

      Before You Begin, make sure you are familiar with Algorand and the concept of Algorand Accounts.

      Key points:
      • Pera Wallet is a container for your Algorand Accounts
      • Pera Wallet allows you to create and manage Algorand Accounts, but the accounts exist on the blockchain, not "on Pera"
      • Pera Wallet is a non-custodial wallet. Any accounts created using Pera remain your responsibility and Pera cannot help you if you lose access to your accounts.

      Step 1: Download the Pera Algo Wallet

      -Download the Pera Algo Wallet application onto your mobile device directly from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Once downloaded onto your mobile device, proceed to Step 2! 

      Step 2: Creating a new Algorand Account on Pera Mobile

      2A: If you are brand new to Pera, select "I want to create an account", then select "Create a new account":


          

      2B: Follow the steps to view and store your recovery passphrase. Consult Backing Up Your Recovery Passphrase for more details. You'll need to store it during this process as you will be asked to prove you know it by providing a few of the words at specific positions.

      2C: Name your account. This name is more like a nickname and it only applies on your phone - it will never be used on the blockchain or on any other wallet or website.


      2D: You will now be prompted to set up security features such as a PIN Code or Biometric Authentication. We recommend enabling all the security features available for your device. Please note that if you create a Pera PIN Code, this does nothing beyond managing access to the app on your phone. It has no relationship with other devices and cannot be used to backup or transfer your accounts if they are lost.

      2E: Your new account is ready to use in your Pera Wallet, visible in your Home Screen.

      Step 3: Create and Connect Your Wallet

      -During the onboarding stage shown below: Select "Pera Wallet" 

      Step 4: Wallet Connect

      -Select the "QR Code" tab. 

      - Open your Pera Algo Wallet on your mobile device and select the "Scan QR" button shown on the home screen of the app. 

      - This will open a QR Scanning window which you'll want to point at the QR Code shown on your computer/device screen. 

       

             

       

       

       

       

       

       

       

       

       

      Select the account you'd like to associate with Vesta Equity and then select "Connect". 

       

      Step 5: Great work all done! 

      Great work, you've now completed the Pera Wallet creation and connection to Vesta Equity. From here you'll now be able to Create your first listing.
